Set Up Call Forwarding:

Forward your calls to:

For iPhone:
  1. Go to Settings → Phone
  2. Tap Call Forwarding
  3. Turn it On
  4. Enter: +16316417727
For Android:
  1. Open Phone app
  2. Tap ⋮ → Settings
  3. Tap Call forwarding
  4. Enter: +16316417727
Test it! Have someone call your number. They should hear CallBunker ask for the PIN (1122) or verbal code ("open sesame").
