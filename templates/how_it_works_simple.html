Important: Protection Only Covers Your Google Voice Number

CallBunker screens calls that come through your Google Voice number. If someone has your real/direct phone number, they can still call you directly and bypass the screening.

Best Protection: Give out ONLY your Google Voice number and keep your real number private.

Legitimate Caller
  1. Calls your Google Voice number
  2. Gets forwarded to your Defense Number
  3. Enters correct PIN (1122) or says "open sesame"
  4. Call connects to your real phone
  5. Gets auto-whitelisted for future calls
After first successful call, they bypass screening next time!
Spam/Robocaller
  1. Calls your Google Voice number
  2. Gets forwarded to your Defense Number
  3. Can't enter PIN or say verbal code
  4. Call gets terminated or sent to voicemail
  5. Gets rate-limited after multiple attempts
Calls to your Google Voice number get blocked - spam can't reach you through this protected line!
⚠️ CRITICAL: Forwarding Setup

In Google Voice settings, forward calls to your Defense Number (assigned by CallBunker), NOT your real phone number. This is essential for call screening to work!

Smart Auto-Whitelist Feature
How It Learns
  • When someone successfully enters your PIN or verbal code
  • CallBunker automatically adds them to your "Trusted Callers" list
  • No manual work required - it happens instantly
  • Works for both PIN and verbal authentication
Future Calls
  • Next time they call, they skip authentication entirely
  • Call connects directly to your real phone
  • No waiting, no PIN entry - instant connection
  • Your contacts only need to authenticate once
Pro Tip: Tell your frequent contacts your PIN (1122) or verbal code ("open sesame") once. After their first successful call, they'll never need it again - CallBunker remembers them automatically!
Full Control: You can remove anyone from your Trusted Callers list in your dashboard if needed.
Important: Making Outgoing Calls
Critical: Always use your Google Voice number for outgoing calls to maintain CallBunker protection!
Three Ways to Make Protected Outgoing Calls:
Google Voice App
Install the Google Voice app and use it for all outgoing calls. Recipients see your Google Voice number.
Call Through Method
Call your Google Voice number first, then dial the person you want to reach when prompted.
Web Interface
Use voice.google.com to make calls from your computer. Perfect for business calls.
Why this matters: If you call from your regular phone, people see that number and call you back directly - bypassing CallBunker protection completely!
