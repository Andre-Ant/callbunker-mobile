Verification Call Not Ringing?

The Problem: Your Google Voice forwards to your Defense Number, which triggers CallBunker authentication before reaching you!
What's Happening
  1. Twilio calls your Google Voice: +1 (617) 942-1250
  2. Google Voice forwards to your Defense Number: +1 (631) 641-7728
  3. CallBunker intercepts and asks for PIN authentication
  4. Twilio (automated system) can't enter PIN, so call fails

Quick Fix Solutions

Option 1: Use SMS Verification (Recommended - No Forwarding Issues!)

SMS goes directly to Google Voice - bypasses CallBunker completely:

  1. In the Twilio verification popup, look for "Send text message instead" or "SMS" option
  2. Click that instead of the "Call me" button
  3. Check your Google Voice for the SMS with verification code
  4. Enter the code in Twilio
Perfect Solution: SMS doesn't trigger call forwarding or CallBunker authentication!

Option 2: Temporarily Forward Directly

Temporarily change Google Voice to forward directly to your real phone:

  1. Go to voice.google.com/settings
  2. Find your current forwarding number: +1 (631) 641-7728
  3. Change it to your real phone: +1 (508) 638-8084
  4. Try Twilio verification again
  5. Important: Change it back to Defense Number after verification!
Google Voice Settings
Option 3: Add Twilio to Whitelist

Add Twilio's verification number to your whitelist (if you know it):

  1. Look at your recent missed calls for a Twilio number
  2. Add that number to your CallBunker whitelist
  3. Try verification again
This is trickier because Twilio uses different numbers for verification calls
After Verification is Complete

Remember to change your Google Voice forwarding back to your Defense Number:

+1 (631) 641-7728
