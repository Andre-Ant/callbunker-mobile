How CallBunker Works

Visual walkthrough of call screening with TextNow

Complete Call Flow

Every call goes through this 4-step screening process

Step 1
Caller Dials

Someone calls your TextNow number that you gave them

Example:
Bob calls:
+1-555-NEW-NUM
Step 2
TextNow Forwards

TextNow automatically forwards the call to CallBunker

Automatic:
Forward to:
+1-631-641-7727
Step 3
CallBunker Screens

Asks caller to enter PIN or say verbal code

Caller hears:
"Please enter your PIN
or say your code"
Step 4
Call Connected

If PIN/code is correct, call forwards to your real phone

Success:
Your phone rings:
+1-555-987-6543
Important: Protection Only Covers Your TextNow Number

CallBunker screens calls that come through your TextNow number. If someone has your real/direct phone number, they can still call you directly and bypass the screening.

Best Protection: Give out ONLY your TextNow number and keep your real number private. This way, all calls must go through screening first.

Legitimate Caller
  1. Calls your TextNow number
  2. Gets forwarded to CallBunker
  3. Enters correct PIN (1122) or says "open sesame"
  4. Call connects to your real phone
  5. Gets auto-whitelisted for future calls
After first successful call, they bypass screening next time!
Spam/Robocaller
  1. Calls your TextNow number
  2. Gets forwarded to CallBunker
  3. Can't enter PIN or say verbal code
  4. Call gets terminated or sent to voicemail
  5. Gets rate-limited after multiple attempts
Calls to your TextNow number get blocked - spam can't reach you through this protected line!
